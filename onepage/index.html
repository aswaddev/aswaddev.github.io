ESCAPE TO AMAZING AT MARGARET RIVER

We invited guests to a unique gourmet food, wine, and travel experience in the Margaret River Region of Western Australia. Guests were led on a series of epicurean adventures, thoughtfully selected to inspire excitement, passion, and the imagination. At the pinnacle were three dining experiences linking each chef’s artistry to the unique qualities of Lexus; ‘Feast in the Forest’, ‘Bold Ingredients, Amazing Flavors’ and ‘Reimagined Wine & Dine' .

FEAST IN THE FOREST

Escape to Amazing opened with an intimate dinner at Leeuwin Estate winery’s rustic Safari Club. As guests huddled around a sizzling fire pit, a few Aboriginal Australian Elders invited the guests onto their land by performing the traditional “Welcome to Country” ritual.

Inside the contemporary log cabin nestled in the local jarrah and marri trees, guests were seated at long communal tables. The feast included locally sourced entrées, vegetable side dishes, and meats cooked over an open fire.

BOLD INGREDIENTS, AMAZING FLAVORS

In a truly extraordinary lunch experience, guests were presented with a uniquely designed and sustainably-sourced spread. Crafted using wild game and foraged natural ingredients from the Margaret River Region and around Western Australia, every dish was exquisitely presented and provided delicious, unexpected flavors.

The culinary experience explored new and unexpected flavors through combinations that pushed the boundaries of culinary experimentation. The lunch was centered around the Lexus guiding pillar of design, and the recognition that brave ideas result in amazing experiences.
